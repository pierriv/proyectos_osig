10 MAR
France's power generation fell by 15% in 2022
French electricity consumption at normal climate declined by 1.7% in 2022, according to the contry's power transmission power operator RTE. This decline in power demand, whih accelerated in the last quarter of 2022 (-9% compared to the average of 2014-2019), was driven by a drop in electricuty consumption by energy-intensive sectors, which were not shielded from tariff increases. Electricity consumption also declined in the residential and services sectors (5% drop in the last four months of the year).
