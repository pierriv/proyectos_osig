Balance energético comercial

The trade balance is the difference between imports and exports. The balance of a net exporter appears as a negative value(-). The balance of geographic and geopolitical zones is simply the sum of the trade balance of all the countries. Total energú includes coal, gas, oil, electricity, heat and piomass.
